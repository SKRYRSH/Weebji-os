PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 2026

WEEBJI OS ("the App", "we", "us") is a gamified self-improvement application available as a Progressive Web App and Android application. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data — email address, used for authentication via magic link (Supabase Auth)
  • Profile data — hunter name, chosen class (Monarch / Mastermind / Monk), avatar selection
  • Activity data — workout logs, focus session records, ritual completions, streak count, level, XP, HP — stored to power the RPG progression system
  • Payment data — plan type (monthly / annual / ghost token), payment status — processed by Razorpay; we do not store card numbers or UPI details
  • Push subscription — browser push endpoint (if you enable notifications) — stored to send streak reminders and system alerts
  • Analytics events — anonymous usage events (e.g. dashboard opened, level up) sent to PostHog to help us improve the app

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and sync your progress across devices
  • To send transactional emails (welcome, streak milestones, payment confirmation) via Brevo
  • To send push notifications for streak reminders and system alerts (only if you opt in)
  • To verify active WEEBJI+ subscriptions
  • To display your rank on the global leaderboard (using your hunter name, never your email)

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL, hosted on AWS). Row-Level Security (RLS) is enabled — each user can only access their own data. Passwords are not used; authentication is exclusively via email magic links.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, edge functions (privacy policy)
  • Razorpay — payment processing for Indian users (privacy policy)
  • Brevo — transactional email delivery (privacy policy)
  • PostHog — anonymous product analytics (privacy policy)
  • GitHub Pages — app hosting
  • Netlify — landing page hosting

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@weebji.com. We will delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.

6. Children's Privacy

WEEBJI OS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at support@weebji.com for any data requests.

8. Push Notifications

Push notifications are entirely opt-in. You can disable them at any time from the Profile screen inside the app, or via your device's notification settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your account or progress in any way.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app banner. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
