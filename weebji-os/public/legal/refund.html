Refund Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

We want you to be happy with WEEBJI+. This policy explains when and how refunds are issued for WEEBJI+ subscriptions.

The short version: If you're not satisfied within 14 days of your first purchase, email us and we'll refund you — no questions asked.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you purchase WEEBJI+ (monthly or annual) and are not satisfied for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial payment. This applies to first-time purchases only.

To request a refund, email weebji@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include the email address used for your account. We will process your refund within 5 business days.

2. Renewal Charges

Once a subscription renews (monthly or annual), the renewal charge is generally non-refundable unless required by applicable law in your jurisdiction. If you wish to avoid a renewal charge, cancel your subscription before the renewal date.

You can cancel at any time by contacting us at weebji@gmail.com or via the billing portal link in your subscription confirmation email.

3. Cancellation

Cancelling your subscription stops future billing but does not delete your account or progress data. You retain access to WEEBJI+ features until the end of your current billing period.

4. Accidental Purchases

If you believe you were charged in error (e.g., duplicate charge, technical issue during checkout), contact us immediately at weebji@gmail.com. We will review and resolve genuine errors promptly.

5. Exceptions

Refunds may be declined if:

  • The 14-day window has passed and the charge is a renewal
  • The account has been suspended or terminated due to Terms of Service violations
  • The request appears fraudulent or abusive

6. Processing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing time may vary depending on your bank or card provider, typically 3–10 business days after we initiate the refund.

7. Contact

All refund requests: weebji@gmail.com

We respond to all emails within 48 hours on business days.
